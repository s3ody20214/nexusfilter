Summary NexusFilter collects minimal data — only what is necessary to provide the service. We do not sell your data to third parties.

1. Data We Collect

Server-side (your game server)

  • IP addresses of connecting players (for rate limiting and banning)
  • Hardware fingerprints (HWID) for account limiting
  • Packet logs (if enabled by you, stored locally on your machine)
  • Player connection events and timestamps

License Activation

  • Server machine identifier (for license binding)
  • License key
  • Activation timestamp

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Player account passwords or personal information
  • Game character data or inventory
  • Payment card details (processed by third-party providers)
  • Your players' real-world identity

2. How We Use Data

  • License validation — To verify your license is active and within limits
  • Signature updates — To push exploit signature updates automatically
  • Support — If you contact us, to help resolve your issue
  • Abuse prevention — To detect and prevent license key abuse

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics profiling, or selling to third parties.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties except:

  • Payment processors — PayPal processes payment data under their own privacy policy
  • Legal requirements — If required by law or court order

4. Data Retention

  • License data is retained for the duration of the license plus 90 days
  • Packet logs stored locally on your server are under your control
  • You may request deletion of your account data by contacting support

5. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests:

  • Email: support@nexusfilter.io
